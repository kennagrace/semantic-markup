This page is going to contain testimonies of Jesus Christ. I'm still working on the details, but for now the following topics are the plan.

Premortal

This section will include testimonies of the Savior in the premortal life.

Scriptures

This section is special in that we have a significant number of written testimonies of people who knew him during his lifetme.

Mortal Ministry

This will be full of testimonies of His mortal ministry.

Resurrected

Just like the others, this section/page will have testimonies of the resurrected Christ.

Here are the definitions for the different pages I will use

  1. Premortal: The Savior before coming to Earth
  2. Mortal Ministry: The Savior's life and Atonement
  3. Resurrected: The Savior after he was crucified and came back from the dead

I hope that I can grow my personal testimony of Jesus Christ during this project!
